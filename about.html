Our Service

At Steadfast, we aim to combine high standards of quality with competitive pricing, service and reliability. To help achieve our objectives, management and workforce retain a close working relationship.

Twenty-four hour, seven days a week manufacturing and a full breakdown service are available. Steadfast have a proven track record, particularly with TAR/shutdown demands, and are able to respond and provide the necessary service to all our customers.

 

  • OVERVIEW

    At the Marla Group of companies, we view ourselves as partners with our customers, suppliers, employees and the community in which we reside. We are committed to operate a policy of environmental preservation in all of our operations, ensuring that this practice is communicated all the way through our supplier chain.

  • COMMITMENT

    Our mission is to maintain a policy of continuous quality improvement in both the products and the services that we provide, placing the company at the vanguard of the industry enabling us to meet the increasingly stringent requirements of the sectors which we serve.



  • AMBITION

    Our goal is to maintain capital investment facilitating the growth and development of our Manufacturing, Stockholding and Distribution network, to evolve an ever improving Project Materials Management service to our clients and to be recognised as one the premier managed supply chain PFF businesses in the world.
